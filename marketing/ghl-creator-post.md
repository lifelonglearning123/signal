## ✅ Posting playbook (from the community-marketing + social skills)

**GHL groups are strict about self-promo — protect reach & avoid removal:**
1. **Post the video natively** (upload to Facebook directly). Native video out-performs a static image and an external link in feed. Use the graphic as the thumbnail/cover or a second image.
2. **No external links in the post body** — links tank reach and trip spam filters. That's the whole point of the "comment a keyword → DM" mechanic.
3. **Check group rules first.** If self-promo is banned, ask the admin for permission to post a paid-gig / hiring post, or post in the group's designated promo/jobs thread.
4. **Reply to every comment fast** (first 1–2 hours). Early engagement = the algorithm shows it to more people. A quick "DMing you now 🙌" on each keyword comment compounds reach.
5. **Then DM applicants** a short brief: what Signal is, the content types you want, how you pay (per deliverable), how to send samples + their rate, and turnaround.
6. **Post to a few groups over several days**, not all at once — same-text blasts across many groups in one day look like spam.

**DM brief template (what to send after they comment):**
> Hey [name] — thanks for the interest! Quick version: Signal is a white-label voice AI agencies resell to local businesses (answers missed calls, books jobs). We're paying creators per project — market rate — for [short-form video / UGC / posts]. We provide footage, screenshots and talking points. Could you send 1–2 samples and your rate for [deliverable]? If it's a fit we'll start with one paid piece and go from there.

**Metrics to watch:** keyword comments per post, comment→DM→reply rate, samples received, first paid piece shipped. Double down on the groups that convert.
