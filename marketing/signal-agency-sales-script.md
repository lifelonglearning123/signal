# Signal — sales video script (selling to a GoHighLevel agency owner)

**Audience:** GHL agency owner = economic buyer → lead with recurring revenue, margin, retention; keep tech light.
**Length:** ~90 sec primary (VSL / paid ad / outreach video / creator-read UGC). 30-sec cut below.
**Structure (sales-enablement arc):** Hook → Problem → Cost → Shift → Walkthrough → Agency ROI → Offer → CTA.

> Two claims to confirm before publishing: the **"up to 1 in 3 calls go unanswered"** stat (widely cited for local businesses — swap in your own source/number), and the **"14 jobs booked"** figure (use a real customer number if you have one).

---

## ▶️ Primary script (~90 sec)

| # | On-screen / visual | Voiceover |
|---|---|---|
| 1 · Hook (0–6s) | Hard cut to text: **"The easiest recurring revenue you're not selling yet."** Phone ringing out. | "If you run a GoHighLevel agency, this is the easiest recurring revenue you're *not* selling yet." |
| 2 · Problem (6–22s) | Split screen: a local business (plumber/dentist) missing a call; a customer hanging up and tapping the next Google result. | "Right now your clients are losing money every day — and they think it's *your* fault. Up to one in three calls to a local business goes unanswered. After hours. On the job. On the other line. And when nobody picks up, that customer just calls the next name on Google." |
| 3 · Cost (22–30s) | Client on a call looking unhappy; text: **"the leads aren't converting."** | "Your client doesn't see a missed call. They see 'the leads aren't converting.' That's how good clients quietly churn." |
| 4 · Shift (30–44s) | Signal UI: an incoming call, AI answering, a calendar slot filling. Your logo on the dashboard. | "Now imagine every client had a receptionist that answered *every* call — day or night — sounded completely human, booked the appointment straight into their calendar, and put hot leads through to a real person. That's **Signal**. And here's the part that matters for you: you deploy it under *your own brand*." |
| 5 · Walkthrough (44–60s) | Quick 3-beat demo: 9pm call → booked; hot lead → transfer to phone; agency dashboard (logo, clients, transcripts, bookings). | "A call comes in at 9pm — Signal answers and books the job while your client's asleep. A red-hot lead calls — it transfers straight to their phone. And you get a dashboard on your own domain: every client, every call transcribed, every booking tracked. To your clients, it looks like *you* built it." |
| 6 · Agency ROI (60–78s) | Text stack: **New service · Your price · Pure margin · No hiring**. A simple "jobs booked" report. | "So you're not just delivering leads anymore — you're *answering* and *booking* them. That's a brand-new service you sell at your own price, pure margin, on top of what you already charge. No hiring. No developers — we run the voice infrastructure, you resell it. And when you hand a client a report that says 'we booked you 14 jobs from calls you used to miss,' they don't cancel." |
| 7 · Offer + CTA (78–90s) | Text: **Your white-label AI voice platform · Unlimited clients · Live in days · $149/mo**. End card with CTA. | "You can have your own white-label AI voice platform live in days — unlimited clients — for a hundred and forty-nine dollars a month. Watch the full demo, or book a 20-minute walkthrough and we'll set it up *with* you." |

## 🎣 Alternative hooks (swap into beat 1 to A/B)

1. **Problem-first:** "Your clients are losing money every day — and they blame your marketing."
2. **Contrarian:** "Stop selling GHL agencies more leads. Sell them the thing that *answers* the leads."
3. **Money-forward:** "One white-label product. Your price. Pure margin. Here's how GHL agencies are adding MRR without hiring."
4. **Curiosity:** "There's a service every one of your clients needs, and almost no agency is selling it."

## 🎬 Production notes
- **Format options:** founder/peer voiceover over screen-recording + B-roll (fastest & most credible); AI avatar via HeyGen/Synthesia if no presenter; or hand this to a creator as a UGC talking-head read.
- **Show real UI** for the walkthrough beat — don't let an AI model hallucinate the product (per the video skill). Use your existing customer clips (`public/videos/*`) for proof.
- **Captions on** (most feed viewing is muted). Keep the on-screen text to the bold phrases above.
- **CTA variants:** paid ad → "Book a walkthrough"; organic FB post → "Comment DEPLOY and I'll DM you a demo"; `/agencies` page → the Deploy button.
